Vision, Mission, Activities and Organization

Our vision is to see children nurtured with biblical and spiritual wisdom so they can serve faithfully in their families, churches and society, and live in a way that honors God.

Our mission is to motivate, mobilize and equip teams of Christian workers who dedicate themselves to teaching and nurturing a new generation to know Christ, experience His healing touch and follow Him faithfully. Equipped workers will minister to families, churches and communities as they engage in the challenges of discipleship through regularly scheduled activities where children will hear God’s word, discover the good news of His kingdom and learn to put God’s love into action. Equippers will divide our ministry into teamsaccording to the type of activities they do and functions they perform:

Governence

Their role is to provide strategic guidance for Equippers, establishing policies, complying with legal obligations, identifying means for support, and providing accountability for the Executive Director. It is presumed that the board members will also help to promote the ministry, network with other organizations and leaders, and serve in various volunteer roles, such as in training and mentoring workers.

Administrative

Financial Administration Human Resources – recruiting and training workers, maintaining files on each worker, etc. Field Administration – Identifying and pioneering new ministry sites, managing teams of volunteers, resourcing workers and ministry sites, resolving conflicts and crises, etc. Ministry Promotion – networking with other ministries, creating brochures, establishing and maintaining website and social media, and mobilizing prayer .

Training team

There will need to be a link between the training team and the HR part of the administrative team, but the training team would be broader than HR, incorporating an array of volunteer and (potentially) paid trainers.
